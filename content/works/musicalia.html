PROJECT OVERVIEW

Musicalia



Project developed in the curricular unit of Digital Games, as part of the Multimedia Master's degree. The game is currently hosted on itch.io at https://epsylonpt.itch.io/musicalia

About the game


Musicalia is a 2D Pixel Art Game that fits into the categories of a RPG, Adventure Game, as well as a Music Rythm Game. The game focuses on helping the main character, Chip, reunite a band and bring color and happiness back to the city of Musicalia. To achieve that goal, the player must solve a series of puzzles to find each band member and convince them to get back together.
